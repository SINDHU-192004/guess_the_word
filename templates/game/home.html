How to play

  1. Enter a 5-letter UPPERCASE English word and submit.
  2. You have a maximum of 5 guesses.
  3. Colors guide you after each guess:
    • Green: letter is correct and in the right position
    • Orange: letter is correct but in the wrong position
    • Grey: letter is not in the word
  4. Win by matching the word; otherwise after 5 tries it’s game over.
  5. You can play up to 3 games per day.
Tip: Use distinct letters in early guesses to learn faster.

About Guess the Word

Guess the Word is a Django 4 (Python) app backed by the Django ORM (SQLite in development). Core models include Game, Word, Guess, and UserProfile; the feedback engine labels each letter as correct, wrong_position, or incorrect. Gameplay rules: 5‑letter words, 5 guesses, with a per‑user daily cap and an optional one‑time Hint exposed via a POST API. Administration is handled through Django Admin and custom dashboards for usage and word management.
